About us

A + Asturias is a new company dedicated to offering customized guided tours in the Principality of Asturias, in the major centres of the region – Oviedo, Gijón and Avilés -, as well as itineraries in the rural and coastal environments. Our services concentrate on the Asturian heritage, both cultural and historical, and in the understanding of natural and environmental values, such as the routes where the main theme is the ethnographic legacy, popular traditions, and the varied and nourishing gastronomy.

A + Asturias is composed of professionals whose extensive training and more than ten years of experience in this sector, both at a national level and in various Central European countries .These guides hold the official title of Tourist Guide for the Principality of Asturias, and a wide knowledge of both the cultural and the natural heritage of the Principality.

In A + Asturias we normally do tours in English, German, French and Spanish, but if you require a tour in a different language, then please don’t hesitate to ask us. At the same time, and in addition to our offer of a variety of itineraries that you can find detailed in our web, we give you the possibility of customized routes depending on your interests and dates, we want to enable you enjoy Asturias with all five of your senses.
