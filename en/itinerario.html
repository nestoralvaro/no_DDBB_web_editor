Itineraries

A+ Asturias will enable you to discover the hereditary wealth, both natural and historical, from the coast to the mountains, the Picos de Europa to the Oscos, and from the incomparable natural to the ethnographic and industrial heritage.

There are many different Asturias’ and with A + Asturias you will be able to discover all of them.

Our Itineraries range over the principal protected natural areas, to each of the regions of the Principality of Asturias, far from the main cities.

Convinced that to know Asturias is not only to know the principal metropolis, in A+ Asturias we offer to guide you to each and every corner of the principality.

You can find the characteristics of our tours to these itineraries by using the menu at the side.
